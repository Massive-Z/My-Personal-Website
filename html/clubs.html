Clubs

I am currently a member of the Penn State Competitive Cyber Security Organization (CCSO), a student-run organization dedicated to educating students on cybersecurity and its many practical applications. The club runs workshops and participates in competitions that provide members with hands-on experience with real-world cybersecurity scenarios.

As a new member, I have not yet had the opportunity to participate in any of the club's competitions, but I am eagerly anticipating the opportunity. My past experiences with a similar competition called National Cyber League was immensely insightful, and I look forward to bringing those skills and enthusiasm to CCSO's events in the future.

I am also excited about attending the club's workshops. My goal is to sharpen my cybersecurity skills and learn how to implement this knowledge in real-world situations. Aside form cybersecurity workshops, the club also offers programming and scripting workshops, which aligns with my interest in expanding my coding knowledge. These opportunities are invaluable as I seek to enhance my abilities and engage more fully with the club's activities.
