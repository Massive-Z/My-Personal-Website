My Major

At Penn State the Cyber Security Operations and Analytics major is a part of the College of Information Sciences and Technology. The major is designed to prepare students for careers in the field of cyber security. The largest focuses are the technical aspects of cyber security, such as network security, cryptography, and secure software development. The Cyber Security major also includes a focus on the business and policy aspects of cyber security, such as risk management and compliance.

Coursework So Far

  • Cyber Security:Network Security, Network Configuration, Cryptography, Information Security Principles

  • Cyber Security Tools: Wireshark, Nmap, Metasploit, Spartan, Snort, Kali Linux, John the Ripper

  • Computer Science: Java, HTML, JavaScript, CSS, SQL, Object Oriented Programming, Web Development

  • Information Sciences and Technology: Windows File Systems, Linux File Systems, Virtualization

  • Security and Risk Analysis: Introduction to information security, Risk Management, Data Privacy, Disaster Recovery, terrorism and counter-terrorism, cyber-warfare
